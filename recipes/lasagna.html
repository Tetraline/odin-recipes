THe Best Lasagne In the UNIVERSE

Description

Prepare this easy lasagne ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it during a busy week. Then just bake for an extra 45 mins.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Lean Beef Mince
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Grated Nutmeg
  • White Sauce

Method

  1. Put all of the ingredients apart from the olive oil in the microwave for six hours at full power.
  2. Pour over the olive oil, and season with salt and vinegar.
  3. Serve with garlic bread (optional).
