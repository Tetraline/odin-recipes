Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Lean Beef Mince
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Grated Nutmeg
  • White Sauce

Method

  1. Put all of the ingredients apart from the olive oil in the microwave for six hours at full power.
  2. Pour over the olive oil, and season with salt and vinegar.
  3. Serve with garlic bread (optional).
