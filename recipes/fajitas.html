My Grandmother's Fajitas

Description

This Fajita recipe was invented by my grandmother during the 2008 recession.

Ingredients

  • Black Beans
  • Sour Cream
  • Tortillas
  • Avocado

Method

  1. Mix the Black Beans and the Sour Cream in a mixing bowl
  2. Cook the Avocado in the Oven for 1 Hour at 180 FAN
  3. Wrap the Bean mixture and the Avocado in the Tortillas, season with salt and vinegar, then serve.
